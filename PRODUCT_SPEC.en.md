# Recoverse Product Spec

## Product Definition

Recoverse is a retrospective capsule app for meeting past versions of yourself.

Users create capsules around a year, life stage, trip, project, relationship, career moment, or any personal theme. Inside each capsule, they answer question cards and build a personal archive they can revisit later.

## Core Experience

> Store memories in a capsule, reopen them later, and rediscover a forgotten version of yourself.

## Core Emotions

- Warmth
- Nostalgia
- Self-understanding
- Mystery
- Quiet discovery

## Primary Users

- People who want to organize year-end retrospectives beautifully.
- People who want to revisit old records and understand themselves.
- People who want to reflect on a specific period, trip, project, or relationship.
- People who may later want read-only sharing with friends.

## Core Structure

```text
Capsule -> Question Card -> Answer
```

## Main Screens

### Home

- Rediscover card
- My capsule list
- Create capsule entry point
- Recent/progress states

### Capsule Detail

- Capsule title and description
- Question card list
- Answer editor
- Progress
- Add/delete question

### Sharing Direction

Initial sharing should be read-only. Login and cloud storage are introduced only when sharing requires them.

## Success Criteria

- The app direction is clearly capsule-first.
- The data structure can later support sharing.
- Home centers on rediscovery and the user's capsules.
- The UI feels like a time capsule, paper letter, and personal archive.
